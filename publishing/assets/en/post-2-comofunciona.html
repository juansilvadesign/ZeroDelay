How ZeroDelay
gets you to real time

1

To avoid stalls, YouTube delivers the live stream with seconds already downloaded ahead — a buffer "cushion", even bigger in 4K.

2

ZeroDelay gently speeds up playback (1.25x) just enough to consume that buffer.

3

You reach the live edge — and it rests on its own. It never speeds up when the buffer is low, so it won't stall.
